Story Telling📖

Once upon a time, in the future, Our protagonist is traveling in space to explore the vast universe as far as possible till his last breath.
The lead saw a planet with a dense forest and refreshing waterfalls, erupting and active volcanoes. He then changed his direction towards the greenish earth like planet.
He named it as "Earth-301🌍" He went near the planet's atmosphere and collected sample of air as he has technologically advanced machines and equipments. In the silent expanse of space, aboard their spaceship, an astronomer conducts research, analyzing air samples collected from a distant planet.
Amidst the hum of machinery and soft glow of instrument panels, they discover tantalizing clues hinting at the potential presence of life, igniting a surge of excitement and wonder.
With his curiosity of having no bounds to know about the planet more widely he tries to enter into the level 1 stage of the atmosphere.
...THEN SUDDENLY....

"Alert! The spaceship has been hit by an asteroid."
With an Emergency warning, the orbitor has been landed with a huge devastating noise and explosion. He slowly boarded the craft with intense fear.
He found no issues with the craft, he assumed its a False alarm that triggered him.
He found a note that describes trespassers will be prosecuted.⚠
