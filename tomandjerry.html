Welcome to the Tom and Jerry Fan Page

Tom and Jerry is a classic animated series created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera. The series centers on the rivalry between the characters Tom, a cat, and Jerry, a mouse. Known for its slapstick humor and enduring appeal, Tom and Jerry has entertained audiences of all ages for decades.

About the Creators

William Hanna and Joseph Barbera were American animation directors, producers, and animators. They co-founded the animation studio Hanna-Barbera, which produced iconic shows like The Flintstones, The Jetsons, and of course, Tom and Jerry.

Did you know?

Tom and Jerry were created in 1940 Tom and Jerry were created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera in 1940. The first episode, "Puss Gets the Boot," was released on February 10, 1940. Tom and Jerry have won numerous awards Tom and Jerry have won numerous awards, including seven Academy Awards for Best Short Subject (Cartoon). They were also inducted into the Cartoon Hall of Fame in 1991.
